2 Alternative Method Based on Volatility Clustering

If the sample is \(<y_1,\ldots,y_n>\) then estimate of variance is built by averaging terms \((y_i - \bar{y})^2\) as \[\hat{\sigma}^2 = \frac{1}{n-1}\sum_{i=1}^n (y_i - \bar{y})^2.\] Make a plot of squared deviations \(z_i=(y_i - \bar{y})^2\).

plot(dat$Input,(dat$Output-mean(dat$Output))^2, type="p",pch=19,
     ylab="Squared Deviations")
